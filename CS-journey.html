My Computer Science Journey

Where It All Began

I started this journey because of a simple CAPTCHA. My interest in Computer Science was sparked during my early years as a QA Engineer. While writing a routine automation test for a login page, I encountered a CAPTCHA that halted the test. This curiosity led me to explore the technology behind it, igniting my passion for the field.

Exploring CAPTCHA Technology

This exploration introduced me to image processing, pattern recognition, and optical character recognition (OCR)—the foundations of CAPTCHA systems. I wondered what other applications this technology could have, opening a new world of possibilities.

Diving into AR and VR

Fascinated by this technology, I delved into Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR). With my 2 years of experience in the game industry as a Game Designer and QA Engineer, I saw the potential to combine Computer Vision with game development for more immersive experiences.
